About Us

Kipini Secondary School was established in 2002 with the aim of providing quality education to students in the coastal region. We have consistently produced top-performing students in national examinations, and we pride ourselves in discipline, hard work, and moral integrity.

  • Vision: To be a center of excellence in academic and moral values.
  • Mission: To provide holistic education to all learners.
  • Motto: Discipline and Hard Work.
